Duthie Day - 10 Dec 2021

Every year, Oxford orthpaedic registrars organise a conference to showcase the local academic and clinical work, and hear from international speakers. The day is named after Robert Duthie, who made great contributions to training and research in Oxford.

Academic Sessions - Part One

11:30-12:00

Chaired by Mr Abtin Alvand

Oxford has a strong commitment to academia, often based at the Botnar. At any given time, about one fifth of our trainees are out of training for dedicated research time, often for a higher degree. This session will showcase some of the work going on in region.
