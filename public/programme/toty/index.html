Duthie Day - 10 Dec 2021

Every year, Oxford orthpaedic registrars organise a conference to showcase the local academic and clinical work, and hear from international speakers. The day is named after Robert Duthie, who made great contributions to training and research in Oxford.

TOTY Nominations

12:45-12:50

Live voting later in the day, with presentations at Duthie Day Dinner

The consultants below have been nominated for Trainer Of The Year. They represent the best of our region and we want to recognise their efforts, as well as emulate these behaviours as consultants ourselves one day.

TOTY voting will take place via an online poll: please input the code on screen at menti.com to cast your vote. Results will be announced at the dinner.
